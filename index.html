About Us

Connecting you with the best of Bangladeshi craftsmanship for a more meaningful shopping experience. Explore a curated selection of high-quality products made with care and precision.

Who We Are

Established in 2014, our journey began from a small workshop in Dhaka. We partnered with local artisans and craftsmen across Bangladesh to bring you products that embody our rich cultural heritage and exceptional craftsmanship. (read more on Our Artisans)

From hand-woven textiles to intricately crafted accessories, each product is made with the highest standards of quality and attention to detail. We source the finest materials locally, ensuring they are sustainable and ethically produced. (read more on Our Materials)

How We Work

We use a direct-to-artisan distribution model focusing on made-to-order and custom services. This approach helps us cater precisely to your needs while minimizing waste and ensuring fair pricing. We believe that meaningful shopping should be accessible and affordable. (read more on Our Approach and Ethical Quality)

Browse our core collection of timeless designs that reflect our passion for quality. Our collection is available on demand, and anything out of stock can be custom made for you within 3-6 weeks. (read more on Made to Order)

Custom Offer

If you have specific requirements or can't find exactly what you're looking for, our custom services are designed for you. With our Custom Made and Made to Measure options, you can personalize our products to fit your exact specifications. Visit us in our Dhaka store for a full experience, or book an online consultation to start your custom project. (read more on Custom Made) (read more on Made to Measure)
